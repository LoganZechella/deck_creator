Weather in May

May is widely considered one of the best times to visit Italy. Temperatures are generally mild and spring-like, warming up steadily as the month progresses.

  • Tuscany (North/Central): Average 60°F - 75°F (15°C - 24°C).
  • Rome & Naples (South): Averages closer to 64°F - 77°F (18°C - 25°C).
  • Packing: "Dress like an onion" (layers). Bring a light jacket for evenings, comfortable walking shoes, and a compact umbrella (early May can occasionally bring brief afternoon showers).

Italo Train Luggage Rules (Rome → Naples)

You booked Italo 9977. Good news: Italo trains allow you to bring luggage for free without strict weight limits.

  • Smart Class limits: If you are in "Smart" class, bags must be under 75 x 53 x 30 cm (29.5" x 21" x 11.8") to fit in the overhead racks.
  • Bigger Bags: If you have massive suitcases, there are dedicated storage racks at the end of each carriage. Try to board early to snag rack space!
  • Security: Always keep a small bag with passports/wallets/valuables on your person or right at your seat.

citizenM Rome Arrival Info

The itinerary notes "check on shuttle". citizenM does not operate its own hotel shuttle.

  • Location: It's beautifully situated in the historic Jewish Quarter, right on the River Tiber, looking out at Isola Tiberina.
  • From Termini Station: Since you arrive by train at 14:33, the easiest group option is grabbing a taxi from the official queue out front. It's only a ~4km (10-15 min) drive and should cost around €15-€20 total.
  • Public Transit option: Bus 'H' goes directly from Termini and drops you right by the hotel in about 19 minutes (€2 per ticket).
